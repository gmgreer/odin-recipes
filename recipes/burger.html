Burger Recipe

An easy to follow burger recipe that even a child can follow. No frills and easy to do for anybody. I prefer avocodo and a in and out style burger sauce but substitute any veg or sauce you like.

Indredients

  • Bread
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • Avocodo
  • Condement

Steps

  1. Toast the bread for best effect, you know how to toast bread right
  2. Make quarter pound patties with the ground beef, 80/20 preferred.
  3. On high heat, Grill the beef 2 minutes per side for 2 flips for a fairly well done burger. Reduce to one minute for a medium rare
  4. Add the cheese after turning off the heat to allow it to melt on burger
  5. Cut tomato and avocod
  6. Put the burger together. Usually bread meat cheese lettuce tomato avocodo
