A decent Espresso

Sadly a decent espresso is more dependant on how good your machine is. Those cheap $100 dollar machines at Wal-Mart or even those espresso knock off nepresso things just aren't capable of making a true espresso. They just can't produce the pressure or consistency of pressure needed to make a good espresso. So first thing do your research and buy a good espresso machine. For a quick video click here. I really like this guys channel and his guides on coffee.

Besides the right machine you just need freshness. Freshly roasted coffee beans, good filtered water. A good coffee grinder helps a lot here to get the fine grind you need for espresso.

Ingredients

  • Filtered Water
  • Freshly roasted coffee

Directions

  1. Click here for a video guide
  2. Grind beans
  3. Put beans in cup
  4. stir coffee to get even distribution
  5. tamp down
  6. tamp down coffee
  7. Use machine and enjoy
